The Framework

Every category revolution
has three pillars.

🧠
Preferences shift
What people want changes
⚡
Technology enables
New tools unlock new scale
💰
Economics align
Value models finally work

When all three converge — a new category is born.

The Analogy

In 2007, Apple didn't make
a better phone.

They built a platform.

Preference People wanted the internet everywhere
Technology 4G networks were ready
Economics AT&T shifted from selling minutes → selling data

The iPhone was 3–5× more expensive than any phone. It outsold them all.

Now look at water

The three pillars are
converging for fine water.

Pillar 1 — Preferences

People are choosing differently.

30% of Gen Z identify as sober or sober-curious
Global wellness market: $6.3 trillion
Premium water growing 8% annually vs 3% for mass market
"Water is the new wine" — not a trend, a generational shift

Pillar 2 — Technology

AI makes what was
impossible, possible.

🍷 An AI water sommelier at every table — educating diners in real time
📋 Dynamic water menus that pair with the chef's tasting menu — automatically
🧪 Mineral profile design — crafting water to complement specific cuisines
📊 Production intelligence — regulatory, quality, logistics at scale

Every restaurant that can't afford a water sommelier... can now have one.
