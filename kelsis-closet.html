📐 Layout Ideas

Common closet configurations — measure your space and pick what fits

📐 L-Shape

L-Shape Layout

Two walls of storage. Long hang on one wall, shelves + drawers on the other. Works for most rooms.

📐 U-Shape

U-Shape Layout

Three walls of storage. Maximum capacity. Needs at least 7ft width for comfortable access.

📐 Galley

Galley (Corridor)

Storage on two facing walls. Great for narrow spaces. Keep center aisle at least 36 inches.

🪵 Materials & Hardware

DIY-friendly options at different price points

Shelving Systems

Budget: IKEA BOAXEL or PAX (~$300-800)
Mid: Elfa from Container Store (~$800-2000)
Splurge: Custom built by carpenter (~$3000+)

Hardware

Knobs: Brass, matte black, or ceramic
Pulls: Bar pulls or cup pulls
Hooks: Brass S-hooks for bags/scarves
Sources: Rejuvenation, CB2, Amazon

Lighting

LED strips: Under-shelf puck lights (~$20-40)
Pendant: Small chandelier or pendant (~$100-300)
Tip: Warm white (2700K) for true fabric colors
